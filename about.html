About Me

Passionate About Data and Learning.

Ever since I was young I have always been curious about the world around me. There are so many complexities in life and I want to learn as much about these complexities. Data, which is a more specific form of information, allows us to analyze and make inferences about this deep web we call life.

I want to levy the powers of data science,machine learning, and software engineering to make processes more effective, to help reduce waste, and to make life for all beings more accessible. With the power and help of these tools, the world will be approaching a new era of technology. And I want to be a part of that revolution and use data to help underprivagled people. This blog will give me the chance to start applying some of the concepts I am learning.

When I am not obsessing over technology, you can find me playing the piano, producing music, learning about neuroscience, lifting and running, reading, and watching anime.
